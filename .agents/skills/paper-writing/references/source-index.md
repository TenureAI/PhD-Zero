# Source Index

## Paper Writing Structure

1. Editage on abstract writing
   - https://www.editage.com/insights/how-to-write-an-effective-abstract-for-a-scientific-paper
   - Used for problem-method-results-implication compression.
2. Paperpal on abstracts
   - https://paperpal.com/blog/academic-writing-guides/research-paper/abstracts
   - Used to reinforce concise summary-first abstract writing.
3. The University of Manchester on introduction sections
   - https://www.manchester.ac.uk/study/masters/blog/how-to-write-a-good-introduction-section/
   - Used for introduction sequencing and reader guidance.
4. Paperpile on related work
   - https://paperpile.com/g/related-work/
   - Used for synthesis-over-summary and grouping-by-idea guidance.
